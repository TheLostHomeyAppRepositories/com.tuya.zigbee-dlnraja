Tuya TS011F Smart Plug

Step 1: Make sure your Homey is in pairing mode for Zigbee devices.
Step 2: Plug the TS011F smart plug into a power outlet.
Step 3: Press and hold the power button on the smart plug for 5-10 seconds until the LED starts blinking rapidly.
Step 4: The device should now appear in Homey's device discovery. Wait for the pairing process to complete.
Note: If pairing fails, try resetting the device by holding the button for 10+ seconds until the LED pattern changes, then repeat the pairing process.
